About these data

Enrollment data comes form the wonderfully easy-to-acess FL Dept of Education.

The plots were created by Adam LaMee. Schools were filtered to those with more than 70 students in a 1st-year biology course, in an attempt to identify high schools, rather than middle or specialty schools. "1st-year" Biology, Chemistry, and Physics courses were identified as typical first exposure in the content area by the course codes for 1, 1 honors, pre-IB, and pre-AICE. For Physics, that also included AP 1, since some schools offer that as a first Physics course. Schools use unique progression plans and the creators of this page do not assume to know what those are on a site by site basis.

Some plots don't appear because they didn't meet the enrollment criteria above.
